Cost of Living in Valencia

Summary of cost of living in Valencia, Spain:

  • The estimated monthly costs for a family of four are 2,983.1$ (2,537.2€), excluding rent (using our estimator).
  • The estimated monthly costs for a single person are 831.9$ (707.5€), excluding rent.
  • Valencia is 8.4% less expensive than Tokyo (excluding rent, see our cost of living index).
  • Rent in Valencia is, on average, 13.2% higher than in Tokyo.
