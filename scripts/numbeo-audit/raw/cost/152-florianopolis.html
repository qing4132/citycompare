Cost of Living in Florianopolis

Summary of cost of living in Florianopolis, Brazil:

  • The estimated monthly costs for a family of four are 2,396.1$ (11,971.5R$), excluding rent (using our estimator).

  • Florianopolis is 49.2% less expensive than Hong Kong (excluding rent, see our cost of living index).
