Cost of Living in Stockholm

Summary of cost of living in Stockholm, Sweden:

  • The estimated monthly costs for a family of four are 4,928.1$ (45,301.4kr), excluding rent (using our estimator).
  • The estimated monthly costs for a single person are 1,370.9$ (12,601.7kr), excluding rent.
  • Stockholm is 42.7% more expensive than Tokyo (excluding rent, see our cost of living index).
  • Rent in Stockholm is, on average, 39.6% higher than in Tokyo.
