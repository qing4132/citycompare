Cost of Living in Kuala Lumpur

Summary of cost of living in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia:

  • The estimated monthly costs for a family of four are 2,240.9$ (8,908.9RM), excluding rent (using our estimator).
  • The estimated monthly costs for a single person are 613.7$ (2,439.7RM), excluding rent.
  • Kuala Lumpur is 3.1% more expensive than Beijing (excluding rent, see our cost of living index).
  • Rent in Kuala Lumpur is, on average, 40.0% lower than in Beijing.
