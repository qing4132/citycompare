Cost of Living in Sao Paulo

Summary of cost of living in Sao Paulo, Brazil:

  • The estimated monthly costs for a family of four are 2,647.7$ (13,228.5R$), excluding rent (using our estimator).
  • The estimated monthly costs for a single person are 716.0$ (3,577.2R$), excluding rent.
  • Sao Paulo is 11.5% more expensive than Beijing (excluding rent, see our cost of living index).
  • Rent in Sao Paulo is, on average, 30.1% lower than in Beijing.
