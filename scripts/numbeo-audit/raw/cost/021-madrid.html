Cost of Living in Madrid

Summary of cost of living in Madrid, Spain:

  • The estimated monthly costs for a family of four are 3,450.4$ (2,934.7€), excluding rent (using our estimator).
  • The estimated monthly costs for a single person are 963.5$ (819.5€), excluding rent.
  • Madrid is 60.2% more expensive than Beijing (excluding rent, see our cost of living index).
  • Rent in Madrid is, on average, 42.6% higher than in Beijing.
