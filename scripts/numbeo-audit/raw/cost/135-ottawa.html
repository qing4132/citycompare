Cost of Living in Ottawa

Summary of cost of living in Ottawa, Canada:

  • The estimated monthly costs for a family of four are 3,868.8$ (5,336.1C$), excluding rent (using our estimator).
  • The estimated monthly costs for a single person are 1,055.8$ (1,456.3C$), excluding rent.
  • Ottawa is 9.7% more expensive than Tokyo (excluding rent, see our cost of living index).
  • Rent in Ottawa is, on average, 25.1% higher than in Tokyo.
