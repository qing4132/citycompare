Cost of Living in Ho Chi Minh City

Summary of cost of living in Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam:

  • The estimated monthly costs for a family of four are 1,642.9$ (43,280,857.3₫), excluding rent (using our estimator).
  • The estimated monthly costs for a single person are 461.4$ (12,155,558.1₫), excluding rent.
  • Ho Chi Minh City is 24.6% less expensive than Beijing (excluding rent, see our cost of living index).
  • Rent in Ho Chi Minh City is, on average, 49.0% lower than in Beijing.
