Cost of Living in Shenzhen

Summary of cost of living in Shenzhen, China:

  • The estimated monthly costs for a family of four are 2,143.9$ (14,643.9¥), excluding rent (using our estimator).
  • The estimated monthly costs for a single person are 578.6$ (3,951.8¥), excluding rent.
  • Shenzhen is 33.5% less expensive than Tokyo (excluding rent, see our cost of living index).
  • Rent in Shenzhen is, on average, 29.5% lower than in Tokyo.
