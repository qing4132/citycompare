Cost of Living in Dublin

Summary of cost of living in Dublin, Ireland:

  • The estimated monthly costs for a family of four are 4,364.5$ (3,712.2€), excluding rent (using our estimator).
  • The estimated monthly costs for a single person are 1,219.3$ (1,037.1€), excluding rent.
  • Dublin is 33.0% more expensive than Tokyo (excluding rent, see our cost of living index).
  • Rent in Dublin is, on average, 127.0% higher than in Tokyo.
