Cost of Living in Amsterdam

Summary of cost of living in Amsterdam, Netherlands:

  • The estimated monthly costs for a family of four are 4,718.2$ (4,013.0€), excluding rent (using our estimator).
  • The estimated monthly costs for a single person are 1,323.3$ (1,125.5€), excluding rent.
  • Amsterdam is 118.3% more expensive than Beijing (excluding rent, see our cost of living index).
  • Rent in Amsterdam is, on average, 127.4% higher than in Beijing.
