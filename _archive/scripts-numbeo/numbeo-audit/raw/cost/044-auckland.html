Cost of Living in Auckland

Summary of cost of living in Auckland, New Zealand:

  • The estimated monthly costs for a family of four are 3,797.3$ (6,467.9NZ$), excluding rent (using our estimator).
  • The estimated monthly costs for a single person are 1,043.7$ (1,777.7NZ$), excluding rent.
  • Auckland is 73.2% more expensive than Beijing (excluding rent, see our cost of living index).
  • Rent in Auckland is, on average, 30.1% higher than in Beijing.
