Cost of Living in Bangalore

Summary of cost of living in Bangalore, India:

  • The estimated monthly costs for a family of four are 1,257.9$ (118,998.5₹), excluding rent (using our estimator).

  • Bangalore is 43.4% less expensive than Beijing (excluding rent, see our cost of living index).
  • Rent in Bangalore is, on average, 64.3% lower than in Beijing.
