Cost of Living in Montevideo

Summary of cost of living in Montevideo, Uruguay:

  • The estimated monthly costs for a family of four are 3,537.0$ (142,356.1$U), excluding rent (using our estimator).
  • The estimated monthly costs for a single person are 985.0$ (39,642.8$U), excluding rent.
  • Montevideo is 23.2% less expensive than Hong Kong (excluding rent, see our cost of living index).
  • Rent in Montevideo is, on average, 73.3% lower than in Hong Kong.
