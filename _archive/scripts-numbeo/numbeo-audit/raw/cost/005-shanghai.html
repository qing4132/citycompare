Cost of Living in Shanghai

Summary of cost of living in Shanghai, China:

  • The estimated monthly costs for a family of four are 2,223.7$ (15,188.9¥), excluding rent (using our estimator).
  • The estimated monthly costs for a single person are 594.4$ (4,059.7¥), excluding rent.
  • Shanghai is 4.0% more expensive than Beijing (excluding rent, see our cost of living index).
  • Rent in Shanghai is, on average, 7.1% lower than in Beijing.
