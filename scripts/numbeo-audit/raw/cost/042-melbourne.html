Cost of Living in Melbourne

Summary of cost of living in Melbourne, Australia:

  • The estimated monthly costs for a family of four are 4,419.7$ (6,230.6A$), excluding rent (using our estimator).
  • The estimated monthly costs for a single person are 1,225.2$ (1,727.2A$), excluding rent.
  • Melbourne is 104.4% more expensive than Beijing (excluding rent, see our cost of living index).
  • Rent in Melbourne is, on average, 73.2% higher than in Beijing.
