Cost of Living in Santiago

Summary of cost of living in Santiago, Chile:

  • The estimated monthly costs for a family of four are 2,686.9$ (2,405,994.9CL$), excluding rent (using our estimator).
  • The estimated monthly costs for a single person are 748.6$ (670,362.6CL$), excluding rent.
  • Santiago is 15.3% more expensive than Beijing (excluding rent, see our cost of living index).
  • Rent in Santiago is, on average, 40.7% lower than in Beijing.
