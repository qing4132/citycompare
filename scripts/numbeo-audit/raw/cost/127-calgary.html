Cost of Living in Calgary

Summary of cost of living in Calgary, Canada:

  • The estimated monthly costs for a family of four are 3,909.1$ (5,391.6C$), excluding rent (using our estimator).
  • The estimated monthly costs for a single person are 1,080.7$ (1,490.5C$), excluding rent.
  • Calgary is 11.7% more expensive than Tokyo (excluding rent, see our cost of living index).
  • Rent in Calgary is, on average, 12.5% higher than in Tokyo.
