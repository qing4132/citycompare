Cost of Living in Hong Kong

Summary of cost of living in Hong Kong, Hong Kong (China):

  • The estimated monthly costs for a family of four are 3,871.7$ (30,320.0HK$), excluding rent (using our estimator).
  • The estimated monthly costs for a single person are 1,078.5$ (8,445.7HK$), excluding rent.
  • Hong Kong is 96.7% more expensive than Beijing (excluding rent, see our cost of living index).
  • Rent in Hong Kong is, on average, 133.2% higher than in Beijing.
