Cost of Living in Brussels

Summary of cost of living in Brussels, Belgium:

  • The estimated monthly costs for a family of four are 4,185.7$ (3,560.1€), excluding rent (using our estimator).

  • Brussels is 90.0% more expensive than Beijing (excluding rent, see our cost of living index).
  • Rent in Brussels is, on average, 33.8% higher than in Beijing.
