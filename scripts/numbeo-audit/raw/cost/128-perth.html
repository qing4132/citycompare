Cost of Living in Perth

Summary of cost of living in Perth, Australia:

  • The estimated monthly costs for a family of four are 4,098.1$ (5,777.2A$), excluding rent (using our estimator).
  • The estimated monthly costs for a single person are 1,130.0$ (1,592.9A$), excluding rent.
  • Perth is 27.1% more expensive than Tokyo (excluding rent, see our cost of living index).
  • Rent in Perth is, on average, 51.1% higher than in Tokyo.
