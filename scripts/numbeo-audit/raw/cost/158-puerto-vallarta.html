Cost of Living in Puerto Vallarta

Summary of cost of living in Puerto Vallarta, Mexico:

  • The estimated monthly costs for a family of four are 3,152.3$ (54,576.9MXN), excluding rent (using our estimator).
  • The estimated monthly costs for a single person are 864.5$ (14,967.6MXN), excluding rent.
  • Puerto Vallarta is 29.3% less expensive than Hong Kong (excluding rent, see our cost of living index).
  • Rent in Puerto Vallarta is, on average, 51.9% lower than in Hong Kong.
