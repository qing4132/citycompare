Cost of Living in Munich

Summary of cost of living in Munich, Germany:

  • The estimated monthly costs for a family of four are 4,362.7$ (3,710.6€), excluding rent (using our estimator).
  • The estimated monthly costs for a single person are 1,265.6$ (1,076.4€), excluding rent.
  • Munich is 106.7% more expensive than Beijing (excluding rent, see our cost of living index).
  • Rent in Munich is, on average, 71.4% higher than in Beijing.
