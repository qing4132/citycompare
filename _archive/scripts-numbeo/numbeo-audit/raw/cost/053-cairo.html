Cost of Living in Cairo

Summary of cost of living in Cairo, Egypt:

  • The estimated monthly costs for a family of four are 1,336.9$ (71,057.2EG£), excluding rent (using our estimator).
  • The estimated monthly costs for a single person are 360.0$ (19,135.4EG£), excluding rent.
  • Cairo is 41.6% less expensive than Beijing (excluding rent, see our cost of living index).
  • Rent in Cairo is, on average, 77.4% lower than in Beijing.
