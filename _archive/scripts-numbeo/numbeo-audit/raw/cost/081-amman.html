Cost of Living in Amman

Summary of cost of living in Amman, Jordan:

  • The estimated monthly costs for a family of four are 2,713.8$ (1,924.1JOD), excluding rent (using our estimator).
  • The estimated monthly costs for a single person are 756.6$ (536.4JOD), excluding rent.
  • Amman is 14.1% more expensive than Beijing (excluding rent, see our cost of living index).
  • Rent in Amman is, on average, 61.7% lower than in Beijing.
