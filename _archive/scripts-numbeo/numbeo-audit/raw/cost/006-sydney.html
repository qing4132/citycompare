Cost of Living in Sydney

Summary of cost of living in Sydney, Australia:

  • The estimated monthly costs for a family of four are 4,570.2$ (6,442.7A$), excluding rent (using our estimator).

  • Sydney is 112.8% more expensive than Beijing (excluding rent, see our cost of living index).
