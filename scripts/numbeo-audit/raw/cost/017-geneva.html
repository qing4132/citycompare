Cost of Living in Geneva

Summary of cost of living in Geneva, Switzerland:

  • The estimated monthly costs for a family of four are 6,761.3$ (5,300.0Fr.), excluding rent (using our estimator).

  • Geneva is 215.2% more expensive than Beijing (excluding rent, see our cost of living index).
  • Rent in Geneva is, on average, 172.2% higher than in Beijing.
